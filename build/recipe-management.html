Advanced Recipe Management

Feature Overview

This feature allows users to manage their recipes more efficiently, using advanced options such as categorization, tagging, and searching.

Key Features

  • Organize recipes into categories
  • Add tags for better searchability
  • Advanced search filters for quick access
  • Save favorite recipes for easy retrieval
  • Share recipes with friends and family

User Guide

To get started, navigate to the recipe management section of the application. You can add new recipes by clicking on the 'Add Recipe' button.
